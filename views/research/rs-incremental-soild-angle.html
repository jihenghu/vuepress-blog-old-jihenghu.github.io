In generating gridded data, only measurements from a single sensor and channel are processed. Measurements have different azimuth and incidence angles (though the incidence angle variation is small). Measurements from multiple orbit passes over a narrow local time may be combined. When multiple measurements are combined, the resulting images represent a non-linear weighted average of the measurements over the averaging period. There is an implicit assumption that the surface characteristics remain constant over the imaging period and that there is no azimuth variation in the true surface TB.
